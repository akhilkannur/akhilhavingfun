5 AI Prompts for Deeper Thinking & Better Output

August 2025

Here are 5 AI prompts that go beyond basic requests, designed to help you think more deeply and generate higher-quality output.

🔹 1. Framework Dissection & Reinvention

Template:
“Analyze the [framework/approach = e.g., Jordan Crawford cannonball outbound].
List its 5–7 core assumptions.
Poke holes in each assumption with real-world counterpoints.
Design a fresh framework that flips these assumptions and gives me 2–3 actionable moves to test.”

Why it works: Forces depth + gives you both critique and new invention.

🔹 2. Philosophy → Unique Content Generator

Template:
“Based on my philosophies around [theme = sales, startups, data tinkering], generate [X] micro-blog ideas.
Style: contrarian, curiosity-driven, and non-generic.
Format: [short punchy lines / numbered list / thread ideas].
Output should feel like it could have been written by me, not generic internet advice.”

Why it works: Anchors in your voice + constraints kill fluff.

🔹 3. Aesthetic Reference Upgrade

Template:
“My current site: [link].
Reference inspiration: [link].
I don’t want a full overhaul. Suggest exactly [3–5] precise changes (colors, fonts, spacing, layout tweaks) that will make it feel premium, modern, and clean—without touching functionality.”

Why it works: You’re asking for surgical moves instead of vague design advice.

🔹 4. Brutal Honesty Diagnostic

Template:
“Evaluate this [idea/site/copy]: [paste].
Be brutally honest—no sugarcoating.
In 3 sentences, tell me the biggest reason this will fail.
Then give me 1 sharper alternative angle to test.”

Why it works: Forces concise critique + immediate alternative.

🔹 5. Self-Positioning Shaper

Template:
“Here’s my current self-description: [paste].
Rewrite it into 3 sharper positioning options that:
Spark curiosity.
Sound confident but not cliché.
Stand apart from typical ‘builder/marketer/founder’ labels.”

Why it works: Gives multiple options + enforces differentiation.
