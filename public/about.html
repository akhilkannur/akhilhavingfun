About Me

I started out in marketing, then spent the last 5+ years figuring things out on my own as a freelancer. I’ve done a bit of everything: sales prospecting, e-commerce management, outreach campaigns, digital products. Some worked, some flopped, all of it taught me something useful.

Along the way I built ProspectingPal to help B2B teams with lead gen, co-founded LeadsBrew to track funded startups, and managed Amazon e-commerce accounts. Right now I’m focused on LISN, where we take live events and real conversations and turn them into video stories people actually want to watch.

The common thread is simple. I like spotting opportunities and turning them into something real, whether it’s data, media, or people’s stories. Now I’m pulling those threads together into media projects and productized services with a small team backing me up.

If any of this connects with you, let’s talk.
